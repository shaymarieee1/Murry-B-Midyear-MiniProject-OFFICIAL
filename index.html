Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff)

Who is Black Widow??

Black Widow, also know as Natasha Romanoff, was one of the most talented spies and assassins in the entire world. She was one of the oringinal members of the Avengers along with Hawkeye, Iron Man, Captain America and Hulk. She's a former agent of S.H.E.I.L.D.. Black Widow was featured in movies including her own, Black Widow, Avengers: Endgame, and Captain America: Civil War.

Fun Factsss!!!

  • Like Captain America, Black Widow is biologically enhanced. So she has superhuman abilities.
  • Based on the MCU, she was born in 1984.
  • She was one of the most talented spies and assassins in the entire world.
